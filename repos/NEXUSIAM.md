## What Is NEXUSIAM?

**NEXUSIAM** is the **pinnacle coordinator** — the hub that connects UNEXUSI to its peers and aggregates the domain repos into a coherent state ready for HODIE.

Where UNEXUSI is the *control center* (tracking what exists), NEXUSIAM is the *living state* — the active orchestration of what's happening *now* across the full ecosystem.

**The step before HODIE:** NEXUSIAM receives from all domain repos, coordinates, resolves conflicts, and presents a unified state to HODIE. HODIE doesn't aggregate — it displays. NEXUSIAM aggregates.

**Prime 11 — Nexus:** Nexus is the connection state. 11 in the prime progression is the coordinator, the Aspect-01, the "all paths lead through here" prime. NEXUSIAM earns its prime.

## The Pinnacle Refinement Connection

NEXUSIAM hosts the **Pinnacle Refinement Workflow** orchestration (from The Yod):

1. Fixed starting message broadcast to domain repos
2. Responses collected from each repo/aspect
3. Similarity check performed
4. Discord flagged if responses converge unexpectedly
5. Workflow triggered to address the discord
6. Result fed to HODIE
