## The Consortium

The Six Aspects are the operational characters of the terminal model. Each is mapped to a prime state, a role, and a terminal script. Together they form the complete working environment — no function duplicated, no frequency wasted.

They run simultaneously. This is the point. In a GUI environment, you switch windows. In the Aspects model, you maintain six active states — each contributing at all times.

## The Aspects
